Privacy Policy for PDF Watch Go

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Nikita Mirashnichenka (nikita.mirashnichenka@gmail.com) ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application, PDF Watch Go (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled, especially regarding our free, ad-supported experience.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

To provide our Service and support our free version through advertising, we and our third-party partners collect certain information.

2.1 Personal Data and Identifiers

While we do not collect traditional personal data (such as your name or email), the Service uses third-party SDKs that may collect:

  • Identifiers: Device identifiers such as the IDFA (Identifier for Advertising).
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app and advertisements (e.g., app launches, taps, and advertising data).
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to ensure the stability of the Service.

2.2 Purpose of Collection

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Third-Party Advertising: To deliver personalized and relevant ads through Google AdMob.
  • Tracking: To measure advertising effectiveness and user engagement across apps and websites.
  • Analytics and Functionality: To monitor app performance, fix bugs, and improve user experience.

2.3 Financial Data

We offer paid subscriptions and in-app purchases. We do not process or store your payment information. All financial transactions are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card numbers or bank details.

3. Advertising and Consent

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Google may use device identifiers to personalize ads based on your interests.

  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT): We will ask for your permission to track your activity. You can manage your preferences or opt-out at any time in your iOS device settings.
  • GDPR Compliance: For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we provide a consent mechanism to manage your privacy choices regarding personalized advertising.

4. How We Handle Your Files (PDFs)

The core function of PDF Watch Go is to transfer documents between your devices.

  • Local Storage: Your PDF files are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad and Apple Watch.
  • Direct Transfer: Files are transferred directly between your devices using Apple’s standard connectivity protocols.
  • No Cloud Uploads: Your documents are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud servers. They remain strictly on your devices.

5. App Permissions

The application may request access to:

  • File System: To allow you to select PDF documents for viewing.
  • Local Network/Bluetooth: To facilitate the transfer of data between your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Tracking: To provide a personalized advertising experience (optional).

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete it from our systems.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
