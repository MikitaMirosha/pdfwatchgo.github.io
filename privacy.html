Privacy Policy for PDF Watch Go

Effective Date: January 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Nikita Mirashnichenka (nikita.mirashnichenka@gmail.com) ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application, PDF Watch Go (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data and how our subscription services work.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. You do not need to create an account with us to use the application.

2.2 Financial Data

We offer paid subscriptions and in-app purchases. We do not process or store your payment information. All financial transactions are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card numbers, billing information, or bank account details.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

The Service allows you to purchase a subscription to access premium features.

  • Payment Processing: Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the confirmation of purchase.
  • Purchase History: Apple may share non-personal transaction data with us (such as the expiration date of your subscription or whether a purchase was successful) solely to allow us to unlock the content you paid for.
  • Apple’s Role: Since all transactions are processed by Apple, they are subject to Apple’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service (https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/).

4. How We Handle Your Files (PDFs)

The core function of PDF Watch Go is to transfer documents between your devices.

  • Local Storage: Your PDF files are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad and Apple Watch.
  • Direct Transfer: Files are transferred directly between your paired devices using Apple’s standard connectivity protocols.
  • No Cloud Uploads: Your documents are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud servers. They remain strictly on your devices.

5. App Permissions

The application may request access to:

  • File System: To allow you to select PDF documents for viewing.
  • Local Network/Bluetooth: To facilitate the transfer of data between your iPhone and Apple Watch.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
