### How it works:
1. Select any PDF file on your iPhone app.
2. Tap "Send to Watch" for instant transfer.
3. Open the app on your Watch and start reading.

### Key Features:
* **Seamless Sync:** Transfer multi-page documents in seconds.
* **Intuitive Controls:** Scroll through text smoothly using the Digital Crown.
* **Smart Zoom:** Double-tap the screen to zoom in on important details.
* **Easy Navigation:** Swipe left or right to flip pages comfortably.
* **Storage Management:** Easily clear old files with a single tap to free up space.

Perfect for students (study notes), travelers (boarding passes and booking confirmations), and professionals (presentations and memos).
Keep your essential documents right on your wrist.
